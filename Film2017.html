My First Roll of Film

I discovered my dad's old film cameras a couple of months back, so I was dying to use them cos I thought they weren't working but they were! It was a lot more complicated than I thought, but it was a good experience.

It was hard to get the right balance of shutter speed and apature at first. Some shots became too dark, too bright, or too blurry, even though I went to "study" how film photography works. But I think it's really important to know all this, especially cos our DSlRs all have auto functions.
