Exploring Home: ArtScience Museum

Thought it was worth putting this up here ^^ Went to all the exhibits ongoing at ArtScience Museum over the past few months!

First up: NASA - A Human Adventure! It's really science-y and I learnt a lot~ And I was also pretty happy with the photos I got. Taking photos of the different metallic structures was quite fun ^^

Everyone's favourite place for step photos and main-worthy shots: Future World - Where Art Meets Science! It was really fun going through the different exhibits~ It was super interactive with places to lie down and watch the art projection and others where you draw on a template and see it come to life! (See my animal truck?)

The last ongoing exhibit is Journey to Infinity: Echer's World of Wonder. Tons of trippy stuff that made me very confused! Gave me a headache too, yikes! But the headache was worth it! But sadly, this exhibit has ended in February 2017!

And here's some shots of the surrounding area! There's Helix Bridge, and generally a good view of Marina Bay Sands and the CBD area! The flowers surrounding the whole of the ArtScience Museum are really pretty too!

Looking forward to new exhibits coming to the museum! ^~^
